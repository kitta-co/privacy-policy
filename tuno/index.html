Privacy Policy

Tuno

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Tuno app ("Tuno") handles information when you use it. Tuno is published by Kitta Co.

No Data Collected by Us

We do not collect user data. We do not operate backend services for Tuno that collect, store, or process information you generate in the app.

AI Features

When you use AI features in Tuno, your data is transmitted only to the AI provider you choose to use for that feature. It is not transmitted to us except as routed directly to that provider.

Data on Your Device

Information you create or import may be stored locally on your device according to how the app and your operating system work. That data stays under your device and account unless you explicitly share it elsewhere using features outside our servers.

Platform and Distribution

Your device manufacturer and app store provider may process certain technical or billing information according to their own policies when you download or purchase apps. That processing is governed by their terms and privacy notices, not by this policy.

Changes

If Tuno's data practices change materially in a future version, we will update this page and, where required, provide notice through the app or store listing.
