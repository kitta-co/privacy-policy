Privacy Policy

HapTick

Last updated: May 11, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the HapTick app ("HapTick") handles information when you use it. HapTick is published by Kitta Co.

Nothing Tracked

Nothing is tracked. HapTick does not collect, track, or transmit your personal data or usage data to our servers.

No Internet Connection Required

HapTick does not require an internet connection to function. The app is designed to work offline.

Data on Your Device

Any settings you configure, such as interval, BPM, or haptic style, may be stored locally on your devices according to how the app and your operating system work. That data stays under your device and account unless you explicitly share it elsewhere using platform features outside our control.

Platform and Distribution

Your device manufacturer and app store provider may process certain technical or billing information according to their own policies when you download or purchase apps. That processing is governed by their terms and privacy notices, not by this policy.

Changes

If HapTick's data practices change materially in a future version, we will update this page and, where required, provide notice through the app or store listing.
